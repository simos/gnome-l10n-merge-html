5.11. Glass Tile

5.11.1. Overview

Figure 16.122.  The same image, before and after applying glass tile effect.

Original image

Filter “Glass Tile” applied


After applying this filter, the active layer or selection is rendered as through a glass brick wall.

5.11.2. Activate the filter

You can find this filter through Filters → Light and Shadow → Glass Tile

5.11.3. Options

Figure 16.123. “Glass Tile” filter options


Preview

Parameter setting results are interactively displayed in preview. Scroll bars allow you to move around the image.

Parameters setting

  • Tile width: Sets tile width (10-50 pixels).

  • Tile length: Sets tile length (10-50 pixels).
